Health Insights

Understanding how we compare your food products.

📈 Health Score

Our proprietary AI score balances nutrient density (proteins, fibers) against processing risks (additives, high sodium). A higher score indicates a more wholesome product.

🧂 Sodium Content

High sodium intake is linked to blood pressure concerns. We flag products that exceed 400mg per 100g as moderate to high risk.

🍬 Added Sugars

Sugar contributes to empty calories. We prioritize products with natural sweeteners or low total sugar counts (under 5g per 100g).

🧪 Processing Level

We analyze the ingredient list for ultra-processed markers like maltodextrin, artificial flavors, and stabilizers that might impact long-term health.
